FAQ

Questions we get every time

Does MyJourney require sponsor approval?

Yes, it's deployed at the study level with full sponsor authorization. We work directly with the sponsor or CRO to configure and validate the platform for each protocol.

How long does implementation take?

Once the protocol summary is approved, MyJourney can be ready for patients in days. No heavy IT integration or infrastructure changes needed.

Does it compromise study blinding?

No. Feedback is fully anonymized and aggregated before reaching the sponsor, structured to protect blinding in accordance with ICH-GCP guidelines.

Is it compatible with our existing tools?

MyJourney is a standalone patient-facing companion. It complements, not replaces, eCOA, EDC, and other clinical tools by focusing on the patient experience layer.

What languages does it support?

Designed for global deployment. Adapts to any country, regulatory framework, and patient population.

What does a pilot look like?

We configure MyJourney for your protocol, deploy it to a defined patient cohort, and deliver blinded feedback reports at agreed intervals. We define success metrics together upfront.
