The patient experience

What it means to finally understand your journey

Imagine being a patient with a rare disease. You've waited years for a treatment. You finally get into a study. And from day one, you have no idea what's going to happen next. Every visit is a mystery. Every procedure is unexplained. You feel alone, and you have no way to say so.

MyJourney changes that. For the first time, patients see their entire study as a clear, visual journey. They understand every procedure before it happens. They can tell someone how they feel, anonymously, privately, without judgment. And if they have a loved one who wants to understand what they're going through, they can share the journey with them too.

The early warning system clinical trials were missing

MyJourney delivers structured, granular feedback from patients, procedure by procedure. Fully blinded, aggregated, in real time.

If 8 out of 12 patients rate a specific procedure negatively across 3 sites, you know before anyone drops out. You can act. You can fix it.

  • ✓ Blinded, aggregated reports. Full ICH-GCP compliance.

All features

Built for patients. Designed for sponsors.

01

Visual journey map

Turns complex timelines into a clear visual pathway. In-clinic and remote visits clearly differentiated. The patient's character walks through their own city of milestones.

02

Plain-language procedure explanations

"What is it?" and "What is it for?" in one tap. Reduces anxiety and improves informed consent quality without losing scientific accuracy.

03

Family Mode

Loved ones follow the same journey map, understand each step, and provide informed support. A unique feature that strengthens the patient's support system.

04

Milestone acknowledgements

Symbolic recognitions at 25%, 50%, 75%, and completion. Shareable milestones that connect patients to the greater purpose of their participation.

05

Anonymous feedback, procedure by procedure

Quantitative ratings and optional qualitative comments. Fully anonymous, aggregated, and structured for sponsors. Blinded intelligence at a level that has never existed before in clinical research.

06

AI-generated blinded reports for sponsors

Structured reports on retention risk, procedure satisfaction, and patient understanding. Powered by AI, fully blinded. Identify dropout signals before it's too late.
